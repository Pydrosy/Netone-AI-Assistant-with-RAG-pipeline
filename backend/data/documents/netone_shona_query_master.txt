# NETONE - COMPREHENSIVE SHONA QUERY MAPPING

## ALL SHONA TOURIST SIM QUERIES MAP TO THESE ANSWERS

### Query: "Tourist SIM inodhura here?"
**Meaning:** Is the Tourist SIM expensive?
**Answer:** SIM pachayo ndeye MAHARA (FREE). Haubhadhare SIM, unobhadhara bundle chete.
**Prices:**  (3GB),  (5GB),  (10GB) - 30 mazuva

### Query: "Ndinowana kupi Tourist SIM?"
**Meaning:** Where do I get the Tourist SIM?
**Answer:** Unogona kuwana Tourist SIM paNetOne shop iri paairport paunosvika muZimbabwe.

### Query: "Ndinoda SIM yevashanyi"
**Meaning:** I want a tourist SIM
**Answer:** Enda kuNetOne shop paairport uine passport yako. Vachakupa SIM mahara.

### Query: "SIM yevashanyi iripi?"
**Meaning:** Where is the tourist SIM?
**Answer:** PaNetOne shop iri paairport.

### Query: "Ndinoda chii kuti ndiwane SIM?"
**Meaning:** What do I need to get the SIM?
**Answer:** Unongoda passport yako yekunze iri kushanda.

### Query: "Ndingaishandisa SIM iyi kwenguva yakareba sei?"
**Meaning:** How long can I use this SIM?
**Answer:** SIM inoshanda kwemazuva 90. Mabundle anoshanda kwemazuva 30.

### Query: "Ko kana ndoda kuwedzera data?"
**Meaning:** What if I want to add more data?
**Answer:** Tenga bundle imwe uchishandisa *401#.
