# NETONE ZIMBABWE - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS & TROUBLESHOOTING

## BUNDLES & USAGE

### Q: How come my bundles are being depleted before their expiry date?
**A:** Our bundles are usage-based. We strongly advise you to monitor your usage. You may need to:
- Monitor your downloads
- Restrict application background data
- Reduce time spent live streaming videos to save your data
- Check your balance regularly using *123# or *171#

### Q: Is there a way I can transfer my data bundles to someone else's account?
**A:** Unfortunately, the option to transfer bundles is not available for now. However, we have alternative facilities:
- **Buy bundles for someone using OneMoney:** Dial *111#
- **Bundle gifting:** Dial *171# and follow the prompts

### Q: How to purchase NetOne bundles?
**A:** Once you have recharged your NetOne SIM card with airtime:

**For USD Bundles:**
- Dial *379#
- Choose "USD Bundles" option
- Select a package of your choice

**For ZWG Bundles:**
- Dial *171#
- Select "Bundles" option
- Choose a package of your choice

### Q: Which bundles does NetOne have?
**A:** We currently have the following bundles:

**Social Media Bundles:**
- WhatsApp bundles
- Facebook bundles
- Instagram bundles
- X (formerly Twitter) bundles

**Voice Bundles:**
- Dollar-A-Day voice bundles
- Khuluma 24/7 voice bundles

**Data Bundles:**
- Pure data bundles (Daily, Weekly, Monthly)
- Big Beautiful Bundles (BBB)
- MoGigs Bundles
- Night Bundle (10pm - 5am)
- Hourly Data Bundles

**SMS Bundles:**
- Daily SMS bundles
- Weekly SMS bundles
- Monthly SMS bundles

**Combo Bundles:**
- OneFusion (Data + Voice + SMS)

### Q: How to check bundle balance on a NetOne SIM card?
**A:** 

**For ZWG Bundle Balance:**
- Dial *171#
- Choose "Balance Enquiry"
- Select the respective bundle

**For USD Bundle Balance:**
- Dial *379#
- Choose "Balance Inquiry"
- Select the respective bundle

**General Balance Check:**
- Dial *123#
- Select "Balance Enquiry"

**Online:**
- Log onto: https://selfservice.netone.co.zw/
- Use the MyNetOne mobile app

## REGISTRATION & DEALERSHIP

### Q: How to become a NetOne dealer?
**A:** Become a NetOne dealer! Sell airtime and SIM cards to earn commissions. Join our dealership program today.

### Requirements for Companies:
- Certificate of incorporation
- Company Summary [formerly: CR 5, CR 6, Memo and Articles of Association]
- ID cards for the directors
- Proof of residence for the directors
- Valid Tax Clearance Certificate
- VAT registration certificate
- Operating License

### Requirements for Individuals:
- National ID card
- Proof of residency
- Tax Clearance Certificate
- Operating / hawkers license

### Q: Where can I get the dealer application forms?
**A:** Visit any NetOne service center or download from www.netone.co.zw/dealers

## TECHNICAL SUPPORT

### Q: My internet is slow, what should I do?
**A:** Try these steps:
1. Check your data balance (dial *124#)
2. Move to an area with better signal
3. Restart your phone
4. Toggle Airplane mode on/off
5. Check if you're in a 4G coverage area
6. Reset network settings if needed

### Q: How do I set up my APN for internet?
**A:** Use these settings:
- APN: netone.internet
- Username: (leave blank)
- Password: (leave blank)
- Authentication type: PAP or CHAP

### Q: I can't make calls but data works, why?
**A:** Your SIM must be fully registered before you can make calls. If already registered, dial 123, disconnect after establishing connection to activate your account.

### Q: How do I check my phone's IMEI?
**A:** Dial *#06#

## ACCOUNT & BILLING

### Q: How do I check my account balance?
**A:** Dial *123# and follow the prompts.

### Q: How do I check my data usage?
**A:** Dial *124# for data balance.

### Q: My account is locked, how do I unlock it?
**A:** This means you have tried to recharge with a wrong or used voucher PIN. Call our Contact Centre on 123 or 121 to get your account unlocked.

### Q: How do I recharge another prepaid account?
**A:** Dial *133*Recharge PIN*Recipient's number# or *171#, select option (6) Airtime Recharge > Select option (2) - Other > Enter Voucher PIN then Confirm.

### Q: I purchased my SIM card recently and I am failing to make calls but data is working. What could be the cause?
**A:** Note that your SIM should be fully registered before you are able to make calls. If already registered, dial 133, disconnect after establishing connection in order to activate your account.
