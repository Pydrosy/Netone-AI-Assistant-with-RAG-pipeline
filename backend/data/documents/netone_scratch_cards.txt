# NETONE ZIMBABWE - AIRTIME & SCRATCH CARD GUIDE

## WHAT IS A SCRATCH CARD?
A scratch card (also called a recharge voucher or airtime card) is a physical card with a hidden PIN that you scratch to reveal and use to add credit (airtime) to your Netone mobile line.

## HOW TO RECHARGE USING A SCRATCH CARD

### Method 1: USSD Quick Recharge
- Dial: **\*133\*PIN#** (replace PIN with the number under the scratch-off area)
- Example: If your PIN is 1234567890, dial \*133\*1234567890#
- You will receive a confirmation SMS once recharge is successful

### Method 2: USSD Menu Recharge
1. Dial **\*171#** from your Netone line
2. Select option **6** for "Airtime Recharge"
3. Select option **1** for "Self" (recharge your own number)
4. Enter the voucher PIN from your scratch card
5. Press Confirm
6. Wait for confirmation SMS

### Method 3: Recharge for Someone Else
1. Dial **\*171#** from your Netone line
2. Select option **6** for "Airtime Recharge"
3. Select option **2** for "Other" (recharge another number)
4. Enter the recipient's phone number
5. Enter the voucher PIN from your scratch card
6. Press Confirm
7. Both you and the recipient will receive confirmation SMS

### Method 4: One-Step Recharge for Others
- Dial: **\*133\*PIN\*RecipientNumber#**
- Example: To recharge 0771234567 with PIN 1234567890, dial: \*133\*1234567890\*0771234567#
- This method recharges the specified number directly

## SCRATCH CARD TROUBLESHOOTING

### "Over Scratched" Card - What to Do

If you have scratched your card too hard and damaged the PIN:

**Problem:** You've scratched off the coating but also damaged some digits of the PIN

**Solutions:**

1. **Check carefully** - Sometimes digits are only partially damaged and can still be read by looking at the shape

2. **Look at the card under good light** - Hold at different angles, the digits might still be visible

3. **Check the card packaging** - Some cards have the PIN printed inside the packaging as well

4. **Visit any Netone Service Center** - Bring the damaged card and your ID. Staff can verify the card and help retrieve the PIN

5. **Contact Customer Care** - Call 111 and explain the situation. They may be able to help if you can provide:
   - The card serial number (usually printed on the card)
   - The purchase receipt (if you have it)
   - Approximate date and location of purchase

### Common Scratch Card Error Messages

| Error Message | Meaning | Solution |
|---------------|---------|----------|
| "Invalid PIN" | The PIN entered is incorrect | Check the PIN carefully, try again |
| "PIN already used" | This card has already been recharged | You may have received a used card, return to point of purchase |
| "PIN expired" | The card has passed its expiry date | Check expiry date on card, return to point of purchase if within date |
| "Network error" | Technical issue with network | Try again after 10-15 minutes |
| "Insufficient balance" | Not applicable for recharge | This error shouldn't appear, contact customer care |
| "Transaction failed" | General failure | Try again, if persists contact customer care |

### Card Purchased But Not Working

If you bought a card and it doesn't work:

1. **Keep the card** - Do not throw it away
2. **Keep the receipt** - If you have it
3. **Return to place of purchase** - Within 7 days with the card
4. **Visit Netone Service Center** - If the seller won't help
5. **Call 111** - Report the faulty card

## AUTO-RECHARGE SETUP

Never run out of airtime with auto-recharge:

1. Dial **\*171\*2#**
2. Select your preferred amount
3. Choose frequency (daily, weekly, monthly)
4. Confirm
5. You'll receive confirmation SMS

To cancel auto-recharge: Dial **\*171\*2\*0#**

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: What is an "over scratched" card?**
A: This is when you scratch too hard and damage the PIN digits, making them hard to read.

**Q: Can I recover a damaged scratch card PIN?**
A: Yes, visit any Netone service center with the damaged card and your ID. Staff can help verify and retrieve the PIN.

**Q: How long are scratch cards valid?**
A: Scratch cards typically have an expiry date printed on them, usually 6-12 months from purchase.

**Q: Can I recharge with a scratch card from another country?**
A: No, Netone scratch cards only work for Netone Zimbabwe lines.

**Q: What if I lose my scratch card before using it?**
A: Unfortunately, lost cards cannot be replaced. Treat them like cash.

**Q: Can I get a refund for an unused scratch card?**
A: Netone does not provide refunds for unused cards, but you can sell/gift it to someone else.

**Q: Why did I get an "Invalid PIN" error?**
A: This could mean: wrong PIN entered, card already used, or card expired. Double-check and try again.

**Q: Is there a fee for using scratch cards?**
A: No, you get the full face value plus any applicable bonus.
