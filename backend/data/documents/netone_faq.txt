# NETONE ZIMBABWE - FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

## ACCOUNT & BILLING

**Q: How do I check my account balance?**
A: Dial *123# from your Netone line. You can also check via the MyNetone app or website.

**Q: How do I check my data usage?**
A: Dial *124# to check your remaining data balance.

**Q: How do I check my airtime balance?**
A: Dial *125# to check your airtime balance.

**Q: How do I get my Netone phone number?**
A: Dial *130# and your number will be displayed on screen.

**Q: How do I buy airtime for someone else?**
A: Dial *128*[amount]*[recipient number]#, e.g., *128*5*0771234567#

**Q: How do I transfer airtime to another Netone user?**
A: Dial *127*[amount]*[recipient number]#, e.g., *127*5*0771234567#

**Q: How do I check my bill for postpaid?**
A: Dial *126# or log into the MyNetone app to view your bill.

**Q: When is my postpaid bill due?**
A: Bills are generated on the 25th of each month and due by the 5th of the following month.

**Q: What happens if I pay late?**
A: A late payment fee of .00 is charged. Service may be suspended after 30 days.

**Q: How do I set up auto-recharge?**
A: Log into the MyNetone app, go to Auto-Recharge, select your preferred bundle and payment method.

## DATA BUNDLES

**Q: What data bundles does Netone offer?**
A: Netone offers daily, weekly, and monthly bundles ranging from 100MB to 100GB. See www.netone.co.zw/bundles for current offers.

**Q: How do I buy a data bundle?**
A: Dial *123*1# and follow the prompts, or use the MyNetone app.

**Q: How do I check my remaining data?**
A: Dial *124# to check your remaining data balance.

**Q: Does unused data roll over?**
A: Standard bundles do not roll over. Selected premium bundles may offer rollover - check bundle details.

**Q: Why is my data finishing so quickly?**
A: Video streaming, app updates, and background data can consume data quickly. Check your data usage in phone settings.

**Q: Can I share my data with family/friends?**
A: Yes, you can gift data via OneMoney or use the data sharing feature in MyNetone app.

**Q: What is 4G+ and do I have it?**
A: 4G+ is enhanced 4G with speeds up to 200 Mbps. Available in major cities if your device supports it.

**Q: How do I set up internet on my phone?**
A: APN settings: APN: netone.internet, Username: (blank), Password: (blank). Save and select this APN.

## ONEMONEY MOBILE MONEY

**Q: What is OneMoney?**
A: OneMoney is Netone's mobile money platform that allows you to send/receive money, pay bills, buy airtime, and more.

**Q: How do I register for OneMoney?**
A: Dial *151# and follow the registration prompts. You'll need your name as per ID and a 4-digit PIN.

**Q: How do I send money via OneMoney?**
A: Dial *151*2#, enter recipient's number, amount, and your PIN.

**Q: How do I check my OneMoney balance?**
A: Dial *151*5# and enter your PIN.

**Q: What are the transaction limits?**
A: Tier 1: /day, Tier 2: /day (with ID), Tier 3: ,000/day (full KYC).

**Q: What are the fees for OneMoney?**
A: Cash-out: 2% (min .20), send to other wallets: .10. Cash-in and bill payments are free.

**Q: How do I change my OneMoney PIN?**
A: Dial *151*6# and follow the prompts to change your PIN.

**Q: I forgot my PIN, what do I do?**
A: Visit any Netone service center with your ID to reset your PIN.

**Q: Can I send money to EcoCash?**
A: Yes, OneMoney is interoperable with EcoCash.

## VOICE & SMS

**Q: How do I buy a voice bundle?**
A: Dial *123*2# and select the bundle you want.

**Q: What voice bundles are available?**
A: Daily (10-60min), weekly (50-300min), and monthly (200-1000min) bundles.

**Q: How do I check my remaining minutes?**
A: Dial *123# and your balance will be displayed.

**Q: How much does SMS cost?**
A: On-net: .05, off-net: .10, international: .20 per SMS.

**Q: How do I buy an SMS bundle?**
A: SMS bundles are included in combo bundles. Standalone SMS bundles are not available.

## ROAMING

A: Dial *126*1# to activate roaming. Do this at least 24 hours before traveling.

**Q: What are the roaming rates?**
A: Rates vary by zone. Zone 1 (SADC): voice .50/min, data .00/MB. Zone 2 (Africa): voice .80/min, data .50/MB.

**Q: Are there roaming bundles?**
A: Yes: 1-day (), 3-day (), 7-day (), 30-day ().

**Q: How do I check roaming rates?**
A: Dial *126*2# to check rates for your destination.

**Q: Can I use my local bundles while roaming?**
A: No, roaming has separate rates and bundles.

## COVERAGE & NETWORK

**Q: Where does Netone have coverage?**
A: Netone covers all provincial capitals and major towns. 4G/LTE coverage: Harare (98%), Bulawayo (97%), Mutare (90%), Gweru (88%), Masvingo (85%).

**Q: How can I check coverage in my area?**
A: Visit www.netone.co.zw/coverage or call customer care at 111.

**Q: Why do I have no signal?**
A: Try restarting your phone, toggling airplane mode, or manually selecting network. If issue persists, check coverage map.

**Q: How do I improve my signal?**
A: Move to a window or higher ground, avoid basements, ensure you're in a coverage area.

## TECHNICAL SUPPORT

**Q: My internet is slow, what should I do?**
A: Check your data balance, signal strength, close background apps, and try resetting network settings.

**Q: How do I reset my APN settings?**
A: Go to Settings → Mobile Networks → Access Point Names, create new APN with: netone.internet (username/password blank).

**Q: I can't send SMS, what's wrong?**
A: Check message center number, ensure you have SMS balance, and try restarting your phone.

**Q: How do I check my phone's IMEI?**
A: Dial *#06# to display your IMEI number.

**Q: How do I update carrier settings on iPhone?**
A: Go to Settings → General → About. If update available, prompt will appear.

## ADDITIONAL SERVICES

**Q: What is call waiting?**
A: Service that notifies you of another incoming call while on a call.

**Q: How do I activate call waiting?**
A: Dial *43# to activate, #43# to deactivate.

**Q: What is call forwarding?**
A: Forwards calls to another number when unavailable.

**Q: How do I set up call forwarding?**
A: Dial **21*[number]# for unconditional, **61*[number]# for no answer, **62*[number]# for unreachable.

**Q: What is voicemail?**
A: Callers can leave voice messages when you're unavailable.

**Q: How do I access voicemail?**
A: Dial 123 from your Netone line.
