# NETONE ZIMBABWE - COMPANY HISTORY & LEADERSHIP

## FOUNDING AND HISTORY
Netone Zimbabwe (officially NetOne Cellular (Private) Limited) was founded in 2005 as Zimbabwe's second mobile network operator.

**Key Milestones:**
- **2005**: Company founded and licensed by POTRAZ
- **2006**: Launched first 2G network
- **2008**: Expanded coverage to all provincial capitals
- **2010**: Introduced 3G services
- **2013**: Launched OneMoney mobile money platform
- **2015**: Rolled out 4G/LTE
- **2018**: Reached 5 million subscribers milestone
- **2020**: Launched fiber-to-the-home services
- **2022**: Upgraded to 4G+
- **2025**: Celebrated 20 years of connecting Zimbabwe

## HEADQUARTERS
Netone Headquarters is located on Sam Nujoma Street in Harare, Zimbabwe.
