### MUBVUNZO: Ndinowana kupi Tourist SIM?
**ENGLISH:** Where do I get the Tourist SIM?
**SHONA:** Ndinowana kupi Tourist SIM?

**MHINDURO / ANSWER:**
- **SHONA:** Unogona kuwana Tourist SIM paNetOne shop iri **paairport** paunosvika muZimbabwe. Tinarwo zvakare kune dzimwe NetOne shops dzose.
- **ENGLISH:** You can get the Tourist SIM at the NetOne shop at the **airport** when you arrive in Zimbabwe. It's also available at other NetOne shops.

### MUBVUNZO: Ndinoda SIM yevashanyi
**ENGLISH:** I want a tourist SIM
**SHONA:** Ndinoda SIM yevashanyi

**MHINDURO / ANSWER:**
- **SHONA:** Enda kuNetOne shop paairport uine passport yako. Vachakupa SIM mahara, wobva watenga bundle uchishandisa *401#.
- **ENGLISH:** Go to the NetOne shop at the airport with your passport. They will give you a free SIM, then you can buy a bundle using *401#.

### MUBVUNZO: Ndinoda chii kuti ndiwane SIM?
**ENGLISH:** What do I need to get the SIM?
**SHONA:** Ndinoda chii kuti ndiwane SIM?

**MHINDURO / ANSWER:**
- **SHONA:** Unongoda **passport yako yekunze** iri kushanda. Hapana ID yekuZimbabwe inodiwa.
- **ENGLISH:** You only need your valid **foreign passport**. No Zimbabwe ID is required.

### MUBVUNZO: Ndingaishandisa SIM iyi kwenguva yakareba sei?
**ENGLISH:** How long can I use this SIM?
**SHONA:** Ndingaishandisa SIM iyi kwenguva yakareba sei?

**MHINDURO / ANSWER:**
- **SHONA:** SIM pachayo inoshanda kwemazuva **90**. Mabundle ako anoshanda kwemazuva **30** ega ega.
- **ENGLISH:** The SIM itself is valid for **90 days**. Your bundles are valid for **30 days** each.

### MUBVUNZO: Ko kana ndoda kuwedzera data mushure memazuva 30?
**ENGLISH:** What if I want to add more data after 30 days?
**SHONA:** Ko kana ndoda kuwedzera data mushure memazuva 30?

**MHINDURO / ANSWER:**
- **SHONA:** Ungangotenga bundle imwe zvakare uchishandisa *401#. Bundle itsva inoshanda kwemamwe mazuva 30.
- **ENGLISH:** You can simply buy another bundle using *401#. The new bundle will be valid for another 30 days.

### MAZWI ANOKOSHA (KEY TERMS)
- **SIM Mahara** = Free SIM card
- **Passport** = Passport (ndiyo chete inodiwa / only required)
- **Airtime** = Mari yekufona / Call credit
- **Bundle** = Hurongwa hwe data, maminitsi ne SMS / Package of data, minutes & SMS
- ***401#** = Kosi yekutenga nekutarisa bundle / Code to buy and check bundle
- **30 mazuva** = 30 days (hupenyu hwebundle / bundle validity)
- **Data** = Internet data
- **Maminitsi** = Minutes yekufona / Call minutes
- **SMS** = Mameseji / Messages
