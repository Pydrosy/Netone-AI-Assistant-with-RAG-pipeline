## HOW TO CHECK BUNDLE BALANCE (ZWG)

To check your ZWG bundle balance:

- **USSD:** Dial *171#, choose "Balance Enquiry", then select the respective bundle
- **Self-Service Portal:** https://selfservice.netone.co.zw/
- **USSD Main Menu:** Dial *123# and select "Balance Enquiry"
- **MyNetOne App:** Check balances in the app dashboard

## BUNDLE USAGE TIPS

- Monitor your downloads to avoid unexpected depletion
- Restrict application background data
- Reduce live streaming of videos to save data
- Check your balance regularly using *171#

## TROUBLESHOOTING

**Q: Why is my bundle being depleted before expiry date?**
A: Bundles are usage-based. Monitor your usage, restrict background data, and reduce live streaming to save data.

**Q: Can I transfer my data bundles to someone else?**
A: Unfortunately, bundle transfer is not available yet. However, you can buy bundles for someone using OneMoney (*111#) or use bundle gifting on *171#.

**Q: How do I check my ZWG bundle balance?**
A: Dial *171#, choose "Balance Enquiry", then select the respective bundle.
