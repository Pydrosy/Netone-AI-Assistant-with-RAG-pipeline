# NETONE - LOCAL LANGUAGE SUPPORT GUIDE (SHONA & NDEBELE)

## COMMON SHONA PHRASES FOR NETONE SERVICES

### Reverse Calling / Zero Balance Calls
- **"164 wena?"** - What is 164? / How do I use 164? (Ndebele)
- **"Handina mari, ndodaidza sei?"** - I have no airtime, how do I call? (Shona)
- **"Ndingafona here ndisina mari?"** - Can I call without airtime? (Shona)
- **"Fona yami ayina mali, ngifoneleni?"** - My phone has no airtime, call me? (Ndebele)
- **"Pay for me inini?"** - What is Pay For Me? (Mixed)
- **"Ndipe mafoni asina mari"** - Give me calls without airtime (Shona)
- **"Ngifonela wena ngizokubhadala"** - Call me and I'll pay (Ndebele)

### Tourist SIM Queries
- **"Ndipe SIM yetourist"** - Give me tourist SIM (Shona/Ndebele)
- **"SIM yevashanyi iripi?"** - Where is the tourist SIM? (Shona)
- **"Ndiri mushanyi, ndoda SIM"** - I'm a tourist, I need a SIM (Shona)
- **"SIM yamavakwasha ikutholakalaphi?"** - Where to get tourist SIM? (Ndebele)
- **"Handina ID yekuZimbabwe"** - I don't have Zimbabwe ID (Shona)
- **"Ngipasepoti kuphela"** - I only have passport (Ndebele)

### Data Bundles
- **"Ndipe data"** - Give me data (Shona/Ndebele)
- **"Data yapera, ndotengei?"** - Data finished, how to buy? (Shona)
- **"Data iphelile, ngithenge njani?"** - Data finished, how to buy? (Ndebele)
- **"Ndinoda bundle"** - I want a bundle (Shona)
- **"Ngifuna ibhandili"** - I want a bundle (Ndebele)
- **"Bundle dzinodhura here?"** - Are bundles expensive? (Shona)
- **"Iyi bundle ingakanani?"** - How much is this bundle? (Ndebele)

### Airtime & Recharge
- **"Ndipe airtime"** - Give me airtime (Shona)
- **"Ngitshele airtime"** - Buy me airtime (Ndebele)
- **"Ndinoda ku recharge"** - I want to recharge (Shona)
- **"Ngifuna ukurecharge"** - I want to recharge (Ndebele)
- **"Scratch card yapera sei?"** - How to use scratch card? (Shona)
- **"Iskhafu kadi isebenza kanjani?"** - How does scratch card work? (Ndebele)

### Network Problems
- **"Network haisi kushanda"** - Network not working (Shona)
- **"Inethiwekhi ayisebenzi"** - Network not working (Ndebele)
- **"Signal yapera"** - Signal gone (Shona)
- **"Isignali iphelile"** - Signal gone (Ndebele)
- **"Ndotadza kufona"** - I can't call (Shona)
- **"Angikwazi ukufona"** - I can't call (Ndebele)
- **"Internet yangu haisi kumhanya"** - My internet is slow (Shona)
- **"I-internet yami ayisheshi"** - My internet is slow (Ndebele)

### Customer Support
- **"Ndinoda rubatsiro"** - I need help (Shona)
- **"Ngifuna usizo"** - I need help (Ndebele)
- **"Ndinokumbirawo kubatsirwa"** - Please help me (Shona)
- **"Ngicela usizo"** - Please help me (Ndebele)
- **"Ndinoda kutaura nemunhu"** - I want to talk to a person (Shona)
- **"Ngifuna ukukhuluma nomuntu"** - I want to talk to a person (Ndebele)

### OneMoney Queries
- **"Ndipe OneMoney"** - Give me OneMoney (Shona)
- **"Ngifuna iOneMoney"** - I want OneMoney (Ndebele)
- **"Ndinoda kutumira mari"** - I want to send money (Shona)
- **"Ngifuna ukuthumela imali"** - I want to send money (Ndebele)
- **"OneMoney yangu haishande"** - My OneMoney not working (Shona)
- **"I-OneMoney yami ayisebenzi"** - My OneMoney not working (Ndebele)

### Service Center Locations
- **"Shop yeNetone iripi?"** - Where is Netone shop? (Shona)
- **"Isitolo seNetone sikuphi?"** - Where is Netone shop? (Ndebele)
- **"Ndinoda shop iripedyo"** - I want a nearby shop (Shona)
- **"Ngifuna isitolo esiseduze"** - I want a nearby shop (Ndebele)
