# NETONE ZIMBABWE - COMPLETE COMPANY GUIDE

## COMPANY OVERVIEW
Netone is a leading telecommunications company in Zimbabwe, providing mobile and internet services to millions of customers across the country. As a key player in Zimbabwe's digital transformation, Netone offers innovative communication solutions for individuals, businesses, and enterprises.

**Founded:** 2005
**Headquarters:** Harare, Zimbabwe
**Coverage:** All 10 provinces of Zimbabwe
**Subscribers:** Over 6 million active users
**Mission:** Connecting Zimbabwe, Enabling Digital Dreams
**Vision:** To be Zimbabwe's most trusted and innovative digital partner
**Parent Company:** NetOne Holdings (Pvt) Ltd
