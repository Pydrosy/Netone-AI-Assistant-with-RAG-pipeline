# NETONE ZIMBABWE - SIM CARD INFORMATION GUIDE

## WHERE TO PURCHASE A NETONE SIM CARD

You can purchase a NetOne SIM card from any of the following locations:

- **NetOne Shops** - Official retail outlets nationwide
- **NetOne Franchisees** - Authorized franchise partners
- **Zimpost Outlets** - Selected Post Office branches
- **NetOne Dealers** - Authorized dealers and agents

**Requirements:**
- Valid National ID card or passport
- USD  or ZWG equivalent (SIM card cost)

🔍 **Find a shop:** Use the NetOne Shop Locator at https://selfservice.netone.co.zw/shop-locator

## SIM CARD COST
A NetOne SIM card costs **USD  or ZWG equivalent**.

## SIM CARD ACTIVATION

### New SIM Card Not Working?
If your new SIM card isn't working, you may need to:

1. **Ensure proper insertion** - Make sure the SIM is correctly placed in the tray
2. **Restart your phone** - Power cycle your device
3. **Check for configuration settings** - See APN settings below
4. **Contact support** if issues persist

## APN SETTINGS (Internet Configuration)

If you're not receiving automatic configuration settings, manually configure your phone:

### For Android Devices:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Mobile Networks** > **More Networks** > **Access Point Names**
2. Tap **Add** or **+** to create new APN
3. Enter the following settings:
   - **Name:** netone
   - **APN:** internet.netone
   - **APN Type:** default
4. Save the settings
5. Restart your phone

### For iPhone Devices:

**Method 1 - iOS 15+**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Cellular** > **Cellular Data Network**
2. Under **Cellular Data**, enter:
   - **APN:** internet.netone
3. Scroll down to **LTE Setup** (if available):
   - **APN:** internet.netone
4. Go back and ensure Cellular Data is enabled

**Method 2 - iOS 14 and earlier**
1. Go to **Settings** > **Mobile Data** > **Mobile Data Network**
2. Under **Mobile Data**, enter:
   - **APN:** internet.netone
3. Scroll down to **LTE Setup**:
   - **APN:** internet.netone

**Important:** Type everything in **small letters** (lowercase).

## CHECKING ACCOUNT BALANCE

### ZWG Balance:
- Dial **\*171#** then select "Balance Enquiry"

### USD Balance:
- Dial **\*379#**

### General Balance Enquiry:
- Dial **\*123#** then select "Balance Enquiry"
- Use the Self-Service Portal: https://selfservice.netone.co.zw

## OUT OF BUNDLE BROWSING

To activate or deactivate Out of Bundle Browsing (OBB):

1. Dial **\*130#**
2. Select "Block OBB" or "Unblock OBB"
3. Restart your device after making changes

## SIM CARD PIN MANAGEMENT

### How to Change SIM PIN:
- Dial: **\*\*04\*Old PIN\*New PIN\*New PIN#**

**Example:** To change from default PIN 0000 to 1111:
- Dial: **\*\*04\*0000\*1111\*1111#**

### Default SIM PIN:
- Usually set to **0000** or **1234** (check your SIM packaging)

### What to do if you forget your PIN:
- Do NOT enter wrong PIN multiple times (SIM will block after 3 attempts)
- If blocked, you'll need a PUK code from customer care

## RECHARGING YOUR SIM CARD

### Using a Recharge Card:
1. Scratch off the panel to reveal the hidden PIN
2. Dial **\*171#**
3. Select "Airtime Recharge"
4. Select "Self"
5. Enter the recharge PIN
6. Confirm

### Using OneMoney:
1. Dial **\*111#**
2. Select "Buy Airtime and Bundles"
3. Choose amount
4. Confirm with your PIN

### Quick Recharge USSD:
- Dial **\*133\*PIN#** (replace PIN with your recharge card number)

## SIM CARD REPLACEMENT

### Requirements for SIM Replacement:
1. **Positive identification** - National ID or valid passport
2. **Full registration** - SIM must be fully registered in your name
3. **Replacement fee** - USD  (or ZWG equivalent)

### Process:
1. Visit your nearest NetOne shop
2. Present your ID
3. Pay the replacement fee
4. Receive new SIM with same number

### If You Lost Your SIM Card:

**For Prepaid SIM Cards:**
- Visit your nearest NetOne shop immediately
- Request SIM card replacement
- Your account will be protected

**For Postpaid SIM Cards:**
1. Call Customer Care immediately to request **Temporary Suspension**
2. Visit a NetOne shop for SIM card replacement
3. Service will be restored on the new SIM

## SIM CARD REPLACEMENT NOT WORKING

If you've done a SIM card replacement but your new SIM is not working, contact Customer Care with the following details:

### Information Needed:
- Your full names
- ID number
- Physical address
- Date of birth
- Phone number of the SIM (the number you're trying to use)
- Serial number on the new SIM card
- Alternative contact number
- Name of the shop where replacement was done

## CAN SOMEONE ELSE REPLACE MY SIM CARD?

**No.** The law requires SIM card replacements to be done only by the registered owner of the SIM card after positive identification.

- You cannot send a friend or relative with their ID to replace your SIM
- The owner must appear in person with their own ID
- This is a security measure to protect your account

## NEW SIM/HANDSET CONFIGURATION ISSUES

If you bought a new SIM or handset and aren't receiving automatic configuration settings:

1. **Manually configure APN** using the settings above
2. **Restart your phone** after configuration
3. **Test internet connection**
4. If still not working, contact customer care

## SIM CARD SECURITY TIPS

- Keep your PIN confidential
- Don't share your PUK code with anyone
- Report lost/stolen SIMs immediately
- Ensure SIM is fully registered in your name
- Buy SIM cards only from authorized dealers
