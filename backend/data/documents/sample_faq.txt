# NetOne Frequently Asked Questions

## Account & Billing
Q: How do I check my account balance?
A: You can check your balance by dialing *123# on your NetOne line, or by logging into the MyNetOne app.

Q: How can I pay my bill?
A: NetOne accepts payments through:
- Mobile money (Airtel Money, MTN Money)
- Bank transfers
- Credit/debit cards via our website
- At any NetOne service center

Q: When is my payment due?
A: Your payment due date is shown on your monthly bill, typically 30 days from the billing date.

## Network & Technical Support
Q: What should I do if I have no network signal?
A: Try these steps:
1. Restart your phone
2. Check if you're in a coverage area
3. Remove and reinsert your SIM card
4. Update your phone's network settings
5. Contact support if issues persist

Q: How do I set up my internet APN?
A: For NetOne internet, use these settings:
- APN: netone.internet
- Username: (leave blank)
- Password: (leave blank)
- Authentication type: PAP or CHAP

## Services & Plans
Q: What internet packages do you offer?
A: NetOne offers:
- Daily bundles (from 100MB to 2GB)
- Weekly bundles (from 1GB to 10GB)
- Monthly bundles (from 5GB to 50GB)
- Unlimited night browsing
- Family share plans

Q: How do I subscribe to a data bundle?
A: Dial *123# and select "Data Bundles", or use the MyNetOne app.

## Roaming
Q: Does NetOne offer international roaming?
A: Yes, NetOne has roaming agreements in over 50 countries. Activate roaming by dialing *123# or visiting a service center.

Q: What are the roaming charges?
A: Roaming charges vary by destination. Check our website for current rates or dial *123# for roaming options.
